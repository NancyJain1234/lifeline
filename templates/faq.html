Why Donate Blood? FAQs

Why is donating blood important?

➕

Donating blood saves lives. Every donation can help up to three people in need of transfusions due to accidents, surgeries, or medical conditions.

Who can donate blood?

➕

Generally, healthy adults aged 18–65 with minimum weight 50kg can donate. Specific eligibility is determined by medical guidelines.

How often can I donate blood?

➕

Whole blood can be donated every 3 months. Platelets and plasma have different intervals. Regular donations help maintain a steady blood supply.

Does donating blood hurt?

➕

Donating blood is safe and causes minimal discomfort. A small needle prick is used, and most donors experience little to no pain.
